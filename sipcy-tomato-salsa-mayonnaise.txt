Makes two cups

5 tomatoes, coared and chopped coarse
0.25 cup fresh-squeezed lime juice
0.25 cup chopped cilantro
1 red onion, diced small
3 jalapenos, finely chopped
Salt and papper to taste

1. Prep ingredients separately and mix well in a metal bowl.  Amount of jalepenos may be reduced for a less spicy salsa.
2. Combine 1.5 cups mayonnaise and 0.5 cup salsa and set aside in refrigerator.
