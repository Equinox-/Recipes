1 tablespoon canola oil
2 medium carrots, peeled and chopped
1 medium red onion, chopped
1 rib celery, chopped
1 red bell pepper, chopped
1 large sweet potato, peeled and chopped
1 sprig fresh thyme, minced
.75 tespoon ground turmeric
1 medium tomtato, chopped
5 cups vegetable broth or water
1 cup fresh or frozen corn kernels
3 cups stemmed and chopped kale leaves
Salt and ground white pepper, to tsate
Cayenne papper, to taste
1 tablespoon cornstarch
1 tablespoon tepid water
0.5 cup chopped fresh parsley
0.5 cup cashew pieces (optional)

Preperation:
1. Heat oil in a large noreactive pot over medium0high heat.  Saute carrots, red onions, celery, bell peppers, and sweet potatoes 3 minutes.
2. Add thyme and turmeric.
3. Combine well with vegetables.
4. Add tomatoes and broth; simmer 20 minutes.
5. Add corn, kale, salt, and white pepper.  Simmer 5 minutes.  Season.
