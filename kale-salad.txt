Kale Salad
Serves 6-8

2 tbsp. currants, dried
7 tbsp. balsalmic vinegar
1 tbsp. rice vinegar (optional)
1 tbsp. honey
1 tbsp. olive oil
1 tsp. salt
2 bunches Tuscan kale, ribs removed, sliced crosswise
2 tbsp. pine nuts, toasted
parmesan

Soak currants in 5 tbsp. balsalmic vinegar, drain
Whisk remaining ingredients into dressing.
Marinate kale in dressing 20 minutes at room temperature.  For tougher kale marinate longer.
