10 minutes preparation, 20 minutes cooking.

Ingredients:
4 coups chicken broth
1 (15 ounce) can coconut bmilk (Thai Kitchen Lite Coconut Milk)
1 tableespoon fish sauce
1 (2-inch) piece fresh ginger, peeled and sliced
1-2 serrano or jalepeno chiles, sliced
1 stalk lemon grass, cut into 1-inch pieces (optional)
2-3 kaffir lime leaves (optional)
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
0.5 pound peeled and deveined shripmp, or 1 (12-ounce) package extra-firm tofu, cubed
0.5 (8-ounce) package sliced cremini or baby bella mushrooms
2 tablespoons fresh cilantro leaves

Method:
Combine first 5 ingredients and, if desired, lemon grass and kaffir lime leaves in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.  Bring to a boil; reduce heat, and simmer 10 minutes.  Stir in lime juice.  Add shrimp, and simmer 10 more minutes.  Discard gigner and lemon grass.  Stir in sliced mushrooms and cilantro.  Makes 7 cups.
